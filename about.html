About Us

DCL Groups was established in April 2006 under the visionary management of Mr. Milin Deng, President and founder. The company initially began its operations in trading, maintenance, and servicing of Press machines and NC Feeder equipment under the name DCL International Co., Ltd.

To accommodate rising market demands and expand manufacturing capabilities, DCL Mold & Auto Parts Co., Ltd. was officially registered on June 13, 2013, with a registered capital of 35,000,000 Baht. Its core business focuses on the professional design and manufacturing of metal stamping dies, as well as providing premium stamped/pressed parts supplying to global leading manufacturers in automotive, motorcycle, and electrical appliance industries.

Today, DCL Groups continues to grow sustainably with a comprehensive production facility footprint of over 11,300 square meters in Thailand and a dedicated workforce of more than 300 professionals. We have strategically expanded our network globally with Auto Wolf Precision Industry in Dongguan, China, and launched a major manufacturing expansion in Thailand under DCL Industry Co., Ltd., backed by a 100,000,000 Baht investment in late 2023.

Our Vision

To be a premier global leader in mold design (Die Making) and high-precision automotive parts manufacturing, driving our partners' success through cutting-edge technology, punctual delivery, and exceptional engineering performance.
